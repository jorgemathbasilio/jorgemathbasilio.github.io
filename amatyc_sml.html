What is the AMATYC Student Math Leauge?

  • The AMATYC Student Math League is a national mathematics competition for two-year colleges.
  • Round 1 is held during the fall semester and round 2 during the spring semester.
  • For more information, please contact Professor Basilio at gbasilio AT mtsac.edu

Quick Info

None is needed. Just show up to the room 15 minutes before the exam begins. Bring a school ID.
Mt SAC students who have not yet earned their two-year degree. High school students are not eligible for the national prizes or the team score, however, they can take the test and compete for the local prizes. For more details you can read the official rules here.
Students are permitted to use any scientific or graphics calculator that does not have a QWERTY (i.e. typewriter) keyboard.
  • 20 questions, 60 minutes, multiple-choice.
  • Challenging questions.
  • Don't expect to finsih the test.
  • A good goal is to answer 10 correct questions (20 points). To be really competitive, you should aim for 15 correct questions (30 points). It is almost impossible to get 20 correct questions (40 points). Do not guess, leave questions blank.
  • Scoring:
    • \(2\) points for each correct answer;
    • \(-0.5\) points for each incorrect answer;
    • \(0\) points for each blank answer.
